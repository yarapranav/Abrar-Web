About This Course

This beginner-friendly course explores the complex climate systems and environmental challenges facing South Asia. From the vital monsoon rains to the impacts of climate change on vulnerable communities, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of environmental issues in one of the world's most climate-sensitive regions.

Through interactive maps, climate data analysis, and case studies, you'll learn about weather patterns, biodiversity hotspots, conservation strategies, and sustainable development practices across South Asian countries.
