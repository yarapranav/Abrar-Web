About This Course

Journey through 5,000 years of South Asian history in this comprehensive course. From the mysterious Indus Valley Civilization to the grandeur of the Mughal Empire, you'll explore the political, social, and cultural developments that shaped the Indian subcontinent.

This course combines archaeological evidence, historical texts, and visual materials to provide a rich understanding of ancient South Asian civilizations. You'll learn about urban planning, trade networks, religious developments, and artistic achievements.

Historical Timeline

3300-1300 BCE

Indus Valley Civilization

Advanced urban civilization with sophisticated city planning, drainage systems, and trade networks.

1500-500 BCE

Vedic Period

Composition of Vedas, emergence of early Hinduism, and development of caste system.

322-185 BCE

Mauryan Empire

First major empire in South Asia, expansion of Buddhism under Ashoka.

1526-1857 CE

Mughal Empire

Islamic empire known for architectural marvels, administrative reforms, and cultural synthesis.
