About This Course

This intermediate course provides a comprehensive analysis of economic development patterns, challenges, and opportunities in South Asia. You'll explore how diverse economies from India to Sri Lanka are navigating globalization, addressing poverty, and pursuing sustainable growth.

Through economic models, case studies, and data analysis, you'll understand the drivers of growth, structural transformations, and policy interventions that shape the region's economic landscape.
