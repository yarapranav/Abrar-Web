About This Course

South Asia is one of the most culturally diverse regions in the world, home to thousands of distinct ethnic groups, languages, religions, and traditions. This course provides a comprehensive exploration of the rich cultural tapestry that makes South Asia unique.

You'll explore everything from ancient architectural marvels to contemporary cultural expressions, understanding how history, geography, and social structures have shaped the diverse cultural landscape of the region.
